    ABOUT US

    Told Plumbing is a family owned business, founded and operated by David and Belinda Told. David and Belinda are 4th generation Pleasant Grove residents. David was raised on the family farm that remains in operation today. Belinda's family comes from a long line of successful business owners. David began plumbing in 1980 and has worked all along the Wasatch Front. David and Belinda attribute their success to quality employees and hard work.
